# DAY15 - AI Secretary

## Overview

DAY15 introduces the first specialized AI employee in Company AI OS: the AI Secretary.

After implementing AI employee switching, the next step is to give each AI employee a specific role. The AI Secretary is responsible for assisting users with daily schedules, task management, and reminders.

This marks the beginning of a role-based AI organization within Company AI OS.

---

## Features

- Design the AI Secretary
- Define the secretary's responsibilities
- Plan schedule management
- Plan ToDo management
- Integrate the AI Secretary with Company AI Chat
- Test the AI Secretary workflow

## Learning Points

A real company assigns different responsibilities to different employees.

Company AI OS follows the same concept by introducing specialized AI employees instead of relying on a single assistant.

The AI Secretary will become the primary interface for managing daily work, schedules, and tasks.

---

## Result

The AI Secretary can now:

- Participate in Company AI Chat
- Handle schedule-related conversations
- Manage task planning
- Serve as the foundation for future calendar and reminder features

This is the first specialized AI employee in Company AI OS.
