# DAY14 - AI Employee Switching

## Overview

DAY14 introduces AI employee switching in Company AI OS.

After building the Company AI Chat, users can now select and communicate with different AI employees through the same chat interface.

Each AI employee maintains its own conversation history, enabling role-specific interactions and creating the foundation for a true multi-agent AI company.

---

## Features

- Create AI employee selection
- Integrate employee switching into Company AI Chat
- Switch between multiple AI employees
- Manage individual Memory for each AI employee
- Test the employee switching workflow

## Learning Points

A single AI assistant is useful, but a company requires multiple specialists.

By introducing AI employee switching, Company AI OS can assign different roles to different AI employees while preserving each employee's own memory and conversation history.

This architecture makes the system modular, scalable, and closer to a real organization.

---

## Result

The Company AI Chat can now:

- Select different AI employees
- Switch conversations seamlessly
- Maintain independent Memory for each AI employee
- Continue conversations based on each employee's history

This is the first step toward building a multi-agent AI company.
