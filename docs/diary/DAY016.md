# DAY16 - Calendar Integration

## Overview

DAY16 focuses on integrating the AI Secretary with a calendar system.

After introducing the AI Secretary, the next step is enabling it to manage schedules. By connecting a calendar, the AI Secretary can register, retrieve, and organize daily events through natural conversations.

This is an important milestone toward building a practical AI assistant inside Company AI OS.

---

## Features

- Design the calendar system
- Register schedules
- Retrieve schedules
- Connect the AI Secretary to the calendar
- Test the calendar workflow

## Learning Points

A digital secretary becomes much more useful when it can access a calendar.

By integrating calendar management, the AI Secretary can understand schedules, answer questions about upcoming events, and prepare for future automation features such as reminders and meeting management.
