Company AI OS - DAY23
Overview

In DAY23, we begin building the Knowledge Base that will become the memory of Company AI OS.

Until now, AI employees could execute assigned tasks, but they could not retain previous knowledge or share information with other AI employees.

This update introduces the foundation for a centralized knowledge storage system that will later support document retrieval, semantic search, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).

The Knowledge Base will become the core infrastructure allowing AI employees to work collaboratively using shared organizational knowledge.

Development Log

The Knowledge Base represents the first step toward giving Company AI OS a persistent organizational memory.

Instead of generating responses from scratch every time, future AI employees will retrieve accumulated knowledge, enabling more consistent, scalable, and collaborative workflows.

This foundation will support document management, semantic retrieval, and intelligent decision-making as the project evolves.
